Valentines Week

Valentines Week is an anthem of love. Takes you to a journey of all loving days of the special week. This one is a must to dedicate to your loving partner.

Koka

Koka is pure punjabi musical classic from Blue Eye Records. This song will compell you to dance on every beat. A must on your playlist if you're punjabi by heart.

Heer

Heer is one of the most popular track of punjabi folk sung by Baljinder Balli. The song takes you to those traditional, melodious culture of Punjab.
